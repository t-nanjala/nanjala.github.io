Projects

The growth and effects of Urban Heat Islands

As a result of infrastructural development, Kenya as a country has suffered major climate change in the past years. As urban development is being encouraged in the country, it is reasonable to study the effects of this and come up with solutions as to how to conquer parts of Kenya becoming UHIs.

Crime analysis and mapping on Thika Superhighway and its environs

With increase in establishments of recreational places along the Superhighway such as night clubs, there has been an increase in fraudlent and conmanship crimes. It is reasonable to study these cases and determine the reasons as to why people fall for these fraudlent tricks and to come up with solutions.

Agricultural land suitability analysis

With climate change affecting the world, Kenya has also suffered losses in terms of agricultural produce. Due to the drought faced in most parts of Kenya, agricultural land analysis can be a great help to farmers preventing major losses to the farmers.
